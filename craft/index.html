01 / 09premium student apartments

Shape the space
you inhabit

A 270-room residence in London's over-supplied student market — a brief to meet ambitious commercial targets in a category fighting for attention against established schemes.

We led with research, gathering academics in psychology, architecture and sociology and listening to students directly. The insight was clear: students who could shape their space were happier. From movable walls to self-curated events to a bespoke soundscape (a world first), every touchpoint laddered up to one line — “Shape the space you inhabit; define the person you become.”
